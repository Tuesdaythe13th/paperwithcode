## Quantum Futures Initiative

Research at the intersection of quantum computing, AI systems, and security assurance. Contributing to quantum futures research through the Open Compute Project (Future Technologies Initiative) with focus on post-quantum cryptography and hardware-rooted security.

## Research Areas

### Post-Quantum Cryptography for AI Systems

Investigating cryptographic frameworks that remain secure against quantum attacks, specifically tailored for:
- AI model protection and integrity verification
- Secure multi-party computation for distributed AI
- Privacy-preserving machine learning in quantum era
- Quantum-resistant authentication systems

### Quantum Data Center Security

Hardware-rooted security architectures for quantum computing facilities and quantum-adjacent AI systems:
- Physical layer security for quantum processors
- Quantum error correction and fault tolerance
- Supply chain security for quantum hardware
- Quantum key distribution (QKD) infrastructure

### Quantum Systems Evaluation

Developing assessment frameworks for:
- Quantum algorithm reliability
- Error mitigation strategies
- Quantum machine learning benchmarks
- Hybrid classical-quantum system verification
